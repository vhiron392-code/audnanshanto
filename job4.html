Oggy and the Cockroaches is a French animated slapstick comedy series about a blue cat named Oggy whose life is constantly interrupted by three mischievous cockroaches: Joey, Marky, and Dee Dee. The show features silent comedy with no dialogue, using only unintelligible vocalizations and gestures.
