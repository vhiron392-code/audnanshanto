About Oggy Theme

Oggy and the Cockroaches is a French animated slapstick comedy series about a blue cat named Oggy whose life is constantly interrupted by three mischievous cockroaches: Joey, Marky, and Dee Dee. The show features silent comedy with no dialogue, using only unintelligible vocalizations and gestures. While Oggy is typically lazy and laid-back, he frequently chases the cockroaches when they torment him, leading to chaotic and hilarious situations. Oggy and the Cockroaches. The design uses sky blue, sunshine yellow, and ocean teal — colors that match Oggy's fun vibe!
